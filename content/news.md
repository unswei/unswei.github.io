### Mar 2026

#### muesli-bt v0.1.0 released

We have released v0.1.0 of **muesli-bt**, a small Lisp-based runtime for robotics behaviour systems.

muesli-bt sits at the centre of a behaviour framework where behaviours are authored in Lisp and executed with clear tick and deadline semantics. The runtime supports bounded-time planning during ticks and explicit asynchronous calls, including cancellation and safe fallback patterns. It is designed to be embeddable and predictable in real systems.

The repository currently includes:

* documentation with runnable demos and screenshots
* structured event logging for inspection and deterministic replay
* CMake install support with exported targets for clean embedding in C++ hosts

Planned next steps include a companion inspection tool, **muesli-studio**, and ROS2 integration.

We are pleased to welcome Prof Frieder Stolzenburg to UNSW Sydney this month.
